## Sample 1

Source note: Expert-revised exemplar supplied by the user as the target "master style" for future academic writing.

Text:
流态化技术是一种以气体或液体作为流体介质，使固体颗粒呈现类似流体状态的独特物料处理方式。其核心特征是流体与颗粒之间复杂的相互作用及其由此导致的颗粒悬浮，对这一现象的研究天然处于多个学科的交汇处，因此流态化本质上是一门高度交叉的学科。
Fluidization technology is a distinctive mode of handling particulate solids in which a gas or liquid serves as the fluid medium, causing solid particles to exhibit fluid-like behavior. Its defining feature is the complex interaction between the fluid and the particles that leads to particle suspension. Naturally, research on this phenomenon sits at the intersection of multiple disciplines, making fluidization an inherently interdisciplinary field.

从学科属性来看，流态化兼具机械工程与化学工程的特征。一方面，流态化过程关注流体与颗粒之间的动力学作用，涵盖颗粒的运动规律、流体流动行为以及两相相互作用的基本机理，属于机械工程的研究范畴。另一方面，流态化几乎渗透于整个过程工业体系，在催化反应、干燥、燃烧、气化、包覆、储能等典型单元操作中均有广泛应用，因此又是化学工程的重要研究对象。就科研与工程实践而言，流态化的理论和应用研究长期由化工背景的学者主导，着重于化工过程优化，并对其中的“机械过程”进行了系统而深入的探索。
From the standpoint of disciplinary attributes, fluidization integrates the contents of both mechanical engineering and chemical engineering. On the one hand, it examines the dynamics of fluid–particle interaction — particle motion, flow behavior, and two-phase interaction mechanisms — thus aligning with mechanical engineering. On the other hand, fluidization permeates virtually the entire process-industry landscape and is widely used in catalytic reactions, drying, combustion, gasification, coating, and energy storage, making it a central topic in chemical engineering. Historically, research and industrial practice in fluidization have often been led by chemical engineers who emphasize process optimization while also pursuing systematic inquiry into the underlying “mechanical processes.”

随着技术进步与产业需求的推动，流态化的应用早已突破传统化工领域，逐渐扩展至医药、环境、材料、生物过程等工程方向。例如，在制药工业中，流态化技术用于造粒与干燥及其药物干粉包衣；在环境工程中，流化床成为固体废弃物处理和污水治理的关键单元；在材料领域中，流化床用于工业制备碳纳米管、电池材料等高附加值材料；在生物过程工程中，流态化生物反应器则展现出优异的传质与反应性能。
Driven by technological advances and industrial demand, applications of fluidization have long transcended the bounds of traditional chemical engineering, extending into pharmaceuticals, environmental engineering, materials science, bioprocess engineering, etc. In pharmaceuticals, fluidization is used for granulation, drying, and dry-powder coating; in environmental engineering, fluidized beds are key units for solid-waste treatment and wastewater remediation; in materials science, they enable the industrial production of high-value materials such as carbon nanotubes and battery materials; and in bioprocess engineering, fluidized bioreactors deliver excellent mass-transfer and reaction performance.

因此，流态化的学习不仅要求掌握机械工程中的流动与力学理论，还需理解化学工程中的反应与过程机制，并能与其他学科建立联系。这种多学科融合的特性，使流态化成为连接基础科学与工程实践的重要桥梁，也对研究者与工程师的知识结构和综合能力提出了更高要求。
Learning fluidization therefore requires mastery of flow and mechanics from mechanical engineering, an understanding of reaction and process mechanisms from chemical engineering, and the ability to integrate knowledge across disciplines. This multidisciplinarity makes fluidization an important bridge between fundamental science and engineering practice, while also placing higher demands on the breadth and integrative capacity of researchers and engineers.

长期以来，系统讲述流态化的中文专著寥寥无几。早期有2001年清华大学出版社出版的《流态化工程原理》和2008年由化学工业出版社出版的《流态化手册》，两部著作内容深邃、体系宏大，对流态化研究具有重要指导意义。然而，它们的理论性较强，对于初入该领域的读者而言，入门门槛相对较高。本书正是在这一背景下编写，旨在以平实而富有启发性的方式，帮助读者全面理解流态化的基本概念与工程原理。
Comprehensive Chinese-language monographs on fluidization have been relatively few. Representative earlier works include Principles of Fluidization Engineering (Tsinghua University Press, 2001) and the Handbook of Fluidization (Chemical Industry Press, 2008). Both are profound in content and broad in scope, offering important guidance to the field; however, their theoretical depth sets a high bar for newcomers. Against this backdrop, the present book aims to present the fundamentals and engineering principles of fluidization in a clear and accessible—yet still rigorous—manner, helping readers form a well-rounded understanding of this important subject.

Style cues:
- Tone: formal, objective, explanatory, and restrained
- Sentence rhythm: mostly medium-to-long sentences with controlled cadence
- Logic: clear progression from definition to scope, applications, classification, and implication
- Lexicon: field-specific, precise, and non-generic
- Rhetoric: smooth transitions without decorative inflation
